强化学习

从基础概念开始，一路接到具身智能里会用到的动作、skill 和 world model。

  1. Reinforcement Learning notes
    从多臂老虎机、MRP、MDP开始，把价值函数和Bellman equation先放到一条线上。

  2. RL notes 2
    动态规划、TD、Sarsa、Q-learning、DQN，以及策略梯度的入口。

  3. RL notes 3 - policy gradient and actor critic
    从REINFORCE、baseline、Actor-Critic接到GAE、TRPO和PPO。

  4. RL notes 4 - offline RL
    记录distribution shift、BC、CQL、IQL，以及它们和机器人数据的关系。

  5. RL notes 5 - continuous control
    连续动作空间下的DDPG、TD3、SAC，以及为什么机器人控制里动作空间设计很重要。

  6. RL notes 6 - model based RL and world models
    从Dyna、MPC、latent dynamics整理到world model。

  7. RL notes 7 - hierarchical RL and skills
    补option、skill learning、goal-conditioned policy和长程任务里的hierarchy。

  8. RL notes 8 - reward modeling and preference learning
    补reward modeling、preference learning、reward hacking，以及它们和机器人反馈的关系。

  9. RL notes 9 - exploration and uncertainty
    补探索、uncertainty、curiosity、sparse reward 和 safe exploration。

  10. RL notes 10 - imitation learning and behavior cloning
    从BC、covariate shift、DAgger写到示范质量，以及它和机器人策略学习的关系。

具身智能

这部分暂时从RL视角往机器人策略学习靠。

  1. Embodied intelligence notes - from RL to robot policy
    先把观测、动作、reward、sim-to-real几个基本问题摆出来。

  2. Embodied intelligence notes 2 - VLA and robot data
    记录VLA、action tokenization、robot data mixture和hierarchy。

  3. Embodied intelligence notes 3 - diffusion policy and action chunking
    接着动作表示往下看diffusion policy、action chunking和temporal ensembling。

  4. Embodied intelligence notes 4 - evaluation and failure modes
    整理具身策略评估里success rate之外更应该看的失败模式、干预成本和安全边界。

  5. Embodied intelligence notes 5 - sim-to-real and deployment gap
    展开domain randomization、system identification、视觉差异、接触动力学和真实部署里的数据闭环。

  6. Embodied intelligence notes 6 - robot foundation models and data flywheel
    从多机器人数据、embodiment metadata、action abstraction 写到失败数据和持续改进闭环。

  7. Embodied intelligence notes 7 - world models for embodied agents
    从latent dynamics、planning、counterfactual、skill-level model 写到 uncertainty 和数据收集。

  8. Embodied intelligence notes 8 - memory and long-horizon tasks
    补长程任务里的 partial observation、task progress、episodic memory、semantic memory 和失败恢复。

  9. Embodied intelligence notes 9 - affordance and object-centric representation
    补 affordance、object-centric representation、keypoints、language grounding 和 action-conditioned perception。

  10. Embodied intelligence notes 10 - human-in-the-loop and intervention learning
    补 demonstration 之后的 human intervention、preference feedback、active query、failure labeling 和 human workload。

  11. Embodied intelligence notes 11 - runtime safety and guardrails
    补动作约束、safety filter、watchdog、fallback、uncertainty gate 和部署日志。
